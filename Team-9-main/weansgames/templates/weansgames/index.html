What Are We About

The campaign is raising funds to buy adapted gaming equipment, consoles and video games for the boys and girls at the children's hospital in Glasgow. Fundraising will also support the hospital's Play Team - so that children in hospital always have someone to play with.

Why Volunteer In Tournaments

Volunteering as a gamer for a kids' hospital is a genuinely meaningful undertaking. It allows you to provide much-needed entertainment and distraction to young patients facing health challenges.
